"a cohesive and inviting brand as fresh as the fish"

We were approached to create an identity for a new fish and chip shop venture. A great restaurant brand starts with a great name, once we'd come up with, 'Charlie Frys', the look and feel of the brand fell in to place as we created a traditional fish and chip shop interior with carefully considered graphics and POS that reinforce the fresh fish feel.
